About Marlco Capital

Building trust through transparency, expertise, and client-focused service since 2011.

Our Story

Marlco Capital Insurance Agency was founded with a simple mission: to make insurance accessible, understandable, and truly protective for Kenyan families and businesses.

In a market often characterized by complex jargon and distant service, we saw an opportunity to do things differently. We built our agency on the principles of transparency, education, and genuine client advocacy.

Today, we serve hundreds of clients across Kenya - from young professionals securing their first motor policy to established SMEs protecting their business assets and families planning for the future with comprehensive life and medical coverage. As the best insurance agency in Kenya, we remain dedicated to your protection.

Why Work With Marlco Capital?

Independent Advice

As an independent insurance intermediary, we're not tied to any single insurer. This means we can shop the market on your behalf, comparing coverage and rates to find the best fit for your specific needs.

Comprehensive Product Range

From motor and medical to life, property, travel, and business insurance, we offer a full suite of products for personal and commercial needs - all under one roof for your convenience.

Proactive Renewal Management

We don't just sell policies and disappear. We actively monitor your coverage, send renewal reminders well in advance, and help you adjust your protection as your life or business evolves.

Claims Advocacy

When you need to make a claim, we're in your corner. We guide you through documentation, liaise with insurers on your behalf, and work to ensure fair, timely settlements.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. We provide clear, itemized quotes and explain exactly what you're paying for, so you can make informed decisions with confidence.

Our Commitment to Kenya

As a Kenyan company, we're deeply committed to serving our local community with excellence and integrity.

We comply with all regulatory requirements set by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) and adhere to the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019) to safeguard your personal information. We partner with top-rated, IRA-licensed insurers to ensure your coverage is backed by financial stability and reliable claims settlement.
