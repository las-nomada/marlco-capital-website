Pension Plans

Build a secure retirement with flexible pension schemes for individuals and employers.

Coverage Options

  • Individual Pension Plans: Personal retirement savings with tax benefits
  • Corporate Pension Schemes: Employee retirement benefits and staff retention
  • Self-Employed Pension: Tailored plans for business owners and freelancers
  • Provident Fund: Lumpsum retirement benefits for employees
  • Retirement Plans: Secure post-retirement income through annuities for guaranteed lifetime payouts or income drawdown for flexible, self-managed withdrawals

Why Plan for Retirement Now?

Start building your retirement fund today and enjoy financial security in your golden years. Kenyan pension schemes offer tax relief and long-term growth.

  • Tax deductions on pension contributions (up to Kshs 20,000/month)
  • Investment growth over time (stocks, bonds, property)
  • Retirement income or lump sum at 50+
  • Death benefits for your beneficiaries
  • Compliance with Retirement Benefits Authority (RBA) regulations

Business Insurance

Comprehensive protection for SMEs and corporate enterprises in Kenya.

Coverage Options

  • Professional Indemnity: For consultants, lawyers, accountants
